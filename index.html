What is this for?

This is a small web application I developed in 2024 while feeling silly about just how long it took me to catalogue things alphabetically. It's been a help to me, and I figured it might be useful for others preparing for positions at their local library, or just wanting to become faster sorters. I've included extra configurations that might fit your use-case, please write suggestions to the GitHub!

If you're looking to commit Dewey Decimal to memory, I suggest Anki!

Select libraries and archives might have unique protocols and requirements for organizing materials, which may differ from general practices (as does mine). If you're unsure, refer to your institution's guidelines.
